About Us

Basti Ki Pathshala is a volunteer-driven NGO committed to providing educational opportunities to children in marginalized communities. Our mission is to ensure every child, regardless of background, gets access to learning, mentorship, and holistic development. We operate with the belief that education is the cornerstone of social transformation, and we strive to empower young minds through compassion, creativity, and consistent community engagement.
